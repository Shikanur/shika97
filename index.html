My chamomile voyage

or how I am falling in love with travelling, nature and learning new things, trying to build my small happy world with big dreams.

In the past I couldn't travel much due to various reasons:

financial problems, study, part-time work, or simply lack of desire.

And I didn't want anything special. but since last year I have been dying just thinking about how beautiful our planet is and I can’t wait to see all the beautiful places on it. and here comes the reason why I want to change my profession, starting to do something completely foreign to me, but which brings me so much pleasure and also gives me the opportunity to travel in the future and at the same time work remotely. Below you can see an incomplete list of things I’ve been trying to do and then places I visited, am crazy about and want to visit no matter the cost.

For a long time I lived without dreams. very long time. The loss of a loved one killed all my desire for life. but now I understand that we
live once and we must live in such a way that there are no regrets later. one must live, not just exist. and after I understood this,
I realized that I wanted to live; my soul became so calm. I really like the way I think now, how I love myself and life.

Camino de Santiago

I just wanna write more about my future Camino :) because I am crazy about it. I wanna walk from Porto to the Camino de Santiago. I’m very strongly drawn to go there, it feels like it will divide my life into before and after. I’m slowly preparing for it, gathering everything I need and myself in spirit.


Why Santiyago de Compostela? Why camino? What am I excepting?

Motivation, with which new doors will open.

I believe I’ll get motivation from this journey. I am purely sure this will be my before/after phase or will give me the strength to believe myself and that I can achieve my goals. Receiving unforgettable energies - I will want to work even harder and see the world.

Wish win my laziness, which prevents me from achieving my goals.

Which places do I go through in order to come to the point? Which difficulties and at the same time what kind of pleasure will it bring? just the thought that I will have the opportunity to go this route gives me enormous motivation and an incredible flow of energy.

Wish just travel/walk through mountains, fields, forests, people.

Usually I am motivated through places, where a lot of old buildings. Historical, especially. Mountains, old towns, nature, parks, fields and places, where few people. Road, me and my thoughts. I saw 100500 photos of the way to Camino de Santiago, so I am 100% sure I will get the best emotions from this adventure.

Did you see and like these wonderful pictures?!

Can anyone really ignore these incredibly beautiful places and this spirit emanating from these places? I can't. I couldn’t and I’m infinitely glad about it. I’m glad that I have such strong willpower and a goal that I strive for and enjoy. Just thinking about it makes me feel like I have wings. It's an incredible feeling.
